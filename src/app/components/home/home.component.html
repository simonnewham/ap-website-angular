Meet Dr Newham

Dr Newham grew up in Pretoria, matriculating at Pretoria Boys' High School in 1977. He studied medicine at the University of Cape Town. His year as an intern was spent in East London at Frere Hospital.

Before starting his specialist training at UCT/Groote Schuur Hospital, he spent time doing general medicine, geriatric medicine, paediatrics and spent a year in Switzerland at the University of Basel...
